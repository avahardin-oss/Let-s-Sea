Who Are We?

R & R

Rest & Renew Collective is a membership-based wellness community designed for high-achieving women navigating burnout and constant pressure to perform. We offer structured, small-group sessions that combine guided reflection with intentional, restorative experiences. These include facilitated book discussions focused on personal growth, hands-on creative sessions such as journaling, vision-setting, and simple craft-based activities, as well as curated conversations led by guest speakers on topics like stress management, mindset, and sustainable performance — with all materials thoughtfully provided. Unlike traditional wellness options, our approach is consistent, community-driven, and designed to fit alongside your career and responsibilities. Each experience is intentionally designed to help members slow down, process their thoughts, and reconnect in a meaningful way. All memberships begin with a brief in-person onboarding to set up scheduling, recurring payment, and ensure the experience is personalized from the start.
